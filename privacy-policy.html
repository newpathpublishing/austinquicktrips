Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Austin Quick Trips ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at austinquicktrips.com and use our services.

What Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Email address: When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is optional and you can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Contact information: If you contact us with questions or feedback, we may collect your name and email address to respond to you.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes information about which pages you visit, how long you spend on pages, and how you navigate our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to track this information.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: Our affiliate partners (Viator, Booking.com, Amazon Associates, GetYourGuide, and TripAdvisor) place cookies on your browser when you click through our affiliate links. These cookies allow them to track your purchases and send us a commission if applicable.
  • Browser and device information: We may collect information about your browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand website traffic and user behavior. Google's Privacy Policy
  • GoHighLevel: We use GoHighLevel for email marketing. Your email address is stored on their servers. GoHighLevel's Privacy Policy
  • Affiliate Programs: Viator, Booking.com, Amazon Associates, GetYourGuide, and TripAdvisor all use cookies to track referrals and commissions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send you our newsletter and travel tips (if you subscribed)
  • To respond to your inquiries and communications
  • To improve our website and user experience through analytics
  • To track affiliate commissions when you purchase through our links
  • To prevent fraud and ensure website security
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and track website usage.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics cookies to understand traffic patterns
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: Used by our affiliate partners to track your purchases

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting business (GoHighLevel for email marketing)
  • Affiliate partners when you click through our links (as required for commission tracking)
  • Law enforcement or legal authorities when required by law

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information is collected
  • The right to know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • The right to delete personal information collected from you
  • The right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in our emails
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of cookies and tracking (with some limitations)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of our website constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
