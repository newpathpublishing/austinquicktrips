Last Updated: April 12, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. These files store information about your browsing behavior and preferences. Some cookies are deleted when you close your browser (session cookies), while others remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them (persistent cookies).

Why We Use Cookies

Austin Quick Trips uses cookies to:

  • Keep the website functioning properly
  • Understand how visitors use our site through analytics
  • Track affiliate commissions when you purchase through our links
  • Remember your preferences for a better browsing experience
  • Provide security and prevent fraud

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas. Without these cookies, the website may not work correctly.

  • Session cookies that track your visit to the site
  • Security cookies that help protect your information
  • Functionality cookies that remember your preferences

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our website. This helps us understand which pages are popular, how long people stay on pages, and how they navigate the site. This information helps us improve our content and user experience.

Google Analytics Information:

  • Data collected: Pages visited, time spent on site, referrer information, and general location data

Affiliate Tracking Cookies

When you click on our affiliate links, partner websites place cookies on your browser. These cookies track whether you make a purchase so the partner company can credit us with the referral. No personally identifiable information is collected through these cookies.

How We Use Cookie Information

Information collected through cookies is used to:

  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior patterns

  • Enhance user experience based on how people use our site

We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information (such as names or email addresses) except as needed for essential site functions or when you voluntarily provide that information to us.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to cookies we place on your device, third-party companies place cookies for analytics and tracking purposes. These include:

  • Google Analytics: Placed by Google to track website usage

We do not control these third-party cookies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information about how they use data.

How to Control and Delete Cookies

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through your browser settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies or specific types of cookies
  • Delete existing cookies from your device
  • Set your browser to warn you when a website tries to place a cookie
  • Only accept cookies from websites you trust

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy and Security > Clear browsing data

Google Analytics Opt-Out

You can prevent Google Analytics from tracking your visits by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on available at Google's opt-out page.

Important Notes About Disabling Cookies

If you block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not function properly. For example:

  • Affiliate tracking may not work correctly if you clear cookies between clicking our link and making a purchase
  • Some website functionality may be limited
  • You may need to re-enter information each time you visit

Essential cookies cannot be disabled without affecting website functionality.
